Crown 0.59 is out! This is a massive update with many rendering-related additions (spotlight
shadows, global lighting, bloom, MSAA to name a few), a new physics Mover for controlling
characters, improved 3D sound spatialization, redesigned scripting component callbacks, a new
Writing Gameplay manual section and the usual batch of bug fixes.

Moreover, the 01-physics sample has been updated to showcase several new features. Play it on your
browser at [https://www.crownengine.org/play](https://www.crownengine.org/play).
